About Mahjong

Mahjong is a classic tile-matching puzzle game that combines patience, skill, and strategy. Match identical tiles to remove them from the board, but remember: only free tiles (those with at least one side open) can be selected. This rule adds an extra layer of strategic thinking to this timeless game.

How to Play Mahjong

Click or tap on matching pairs of tiles to remove them from the board. Tiles can only be selected if they are free (not covered by other tiles and have at least one side open). Clear all tiles to win! The game features relaxing background music and satisfying tile-click sound effects for an immersive experience.

Strategic Gameplay

Think ahead and plan your moves carefully. The order in which you remove tiles can make the difference between success and getting stuck!

Beautiful Design

Enjoy traditional Chinese tile designs with authentic symbols and patterns. The game features both classic and modern visual styles.

Helpful Features

Use the hint system, shuffle option, and undo feature to help you when you're stuck. Perfect for both beginners and experienced players!

Relaxing Experience

Immerse yourself in the soothing atmosphere with traditional Chinese music and satisfying sound effects. A perfect way to unwind!

About Block Blast

Block Blast is an addictive puzzle game that challenges your pattern recognition and strategic thinking. Match similar blocks, create powerful combos, and aim for the highest score possible in this brain-teasing adventure!

How to Play Block Blast

The rules are simple yet mastering them requires skill. Tap or click on groups of two or more identical blocks to remove them from the board. The larger the group, the more points you earn! Special blocks and power-ups will appear as you progress, opening up exciting strategic possibilities.

Strategic Gameplay

Plan your moves carefully to create chain reactions and maximize your score. Think ahead and develop winning strategies!

Colorful Challenges

Navigate through increasingly difficult levels with vibrant blocks and exciting challenges that will keep you engaged for hours.

Power-Ups

Brain Training

Improve your cognitive skills including pattern recognition, spatial awareness, and quick decision making while having fun!

About Sudoku

Sudoku is a classic number puzzle game that challenges your logical thinking and problem-solving abilities. Fill in the grid with numbers 1-9, ensuring each row, column, and 3x3 box contains all digits without repetition.

How to Play Sudoku

Start with the pre-filled numbers and use logic to fill in the remaining cells. Each row, column, and 3x3 box must contain the numbers 1-9 without repeating any number. Use the process of elimination and deduction to solve the puzzle!

About Mathematical Crossword

Mathematical Crossword is a unique puzzle game that combines the classic crossword format with mathematical operations. Place numbers and arithmetic symbols on the grid to create valid mathematical equations both horizontally and vertically!

How to Play Mathematical Crossword

Fill in the empty boxes on the grid with numbers and arithmetic symbols given below your screen. You can drag and drop numbers and symbols, or click on an empty cell and select from the available options. Remember that calculations are performed from left to right and from top to bottom. The game will automatically check your answers as you complete the puzzle.

About Wordmeister

Wordmeister is an engaging online word game inspired by Scrabble where you can showcase your vocabulary skills. Create words using letter tiles to earn points, with longer words and strategic placement on bonus squares earning you higher scores.

How to Play Wordmeister

Form words by arranging your letter tiles on the game board, starting from the center and connecting to existing tiles. Create crossword-style patterns both vertically and horizontally to maximize your score. Play against a computer opponent and aim to have the highest score by the end of the game.

Strategic Gameplay

Utilize bonus squares to multiply your points! Blue squares double letter values, green squares triple letter values, red squares double word values, and orange squares triple word values.

Vocabulary Challenge

Test and expand your vocabulary with each play. The game includes an internal dictionary to verify valid words, encouraging you to discover new combinations.

Scoring System

Each letter has a point value, with common letters worth less and rare letters worth more. Plan your moves carefully to maximize points through strategic placement on bonus squares.

Adaptable Strategies

Swap tiles when necessary to improve your hand, potentially setting up for higher-scoring words in future turns. Balance immediate points with long-term strategy.
